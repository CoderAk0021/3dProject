Our Collection

Built-in 2002 (New Library Building), our library is a knowledge hub with over 223,000 books in science, engineering, and technology. We offer many e-books and online journals from top publishers like IEEE and Springer. Through the N-LIST program, our students and faculty can access a wide range of additional e-books and e-journals. Our library is designed to be user-friendly, with easy access to all resources. Whether you are a student or a teacher, you will find everything you need for your studies and research here. Come and explore the world of information at our library.
